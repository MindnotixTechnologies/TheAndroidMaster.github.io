James Fenn > Fornax

Free wallpapers, made by everyone, for everyone.

Fornax is a collection of flat wallpapers made by designers across the world.

All of the wallpapers are created with the intention of making android look good, to create a sense of depth throughout the device. The app features a simple layout, search, and wallpaper preview, with the addition of an offline mode so the wallpapers can be viewed without using data.
